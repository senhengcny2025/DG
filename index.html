Stepping into our DG Area, designed as a vintage living room, you’ll experience the spirit of unity and familial support that laid the foundation for Senheng’s success. In 1989, Senheng was founded by our Chairman KH Lim and his two brothers, KC Lim and KY Lim. Their shared vision and collaboration were key to the company’s early growth. As you enter, the photo wall titled "三十五年 携手共进" serves as a reminder of Senheng’s journey over the years. The vintage setting symbolizes the strength of family support, encouraging us to cherish our family bonds as we continue to grow together.
